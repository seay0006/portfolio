Our Story

When making NilocDevs, Colin Seay was determined to implement the country ideals that he learned when working as a farmer and heavy machine operator. Working long and hard hours so that others could have an easier life.

Wanting to bring those beliefs into the Web Development world he continues to work long hours for his clients with the goal of giving them the website they dreamed of, guaranteeing accessibility he wants people from all paths of life to be able to engage with his work.

Our Goal

NilocDevs aspires to create a change with the clients they take. Hoping to build a name that they can be proud of.

We hope to develop into a big enough company that we can extend our reach wider allowing more people to achieve the online presence that their company deserves.

Finally we intend on helping one small upcoming company in need of web help as often as we can afford, hopefully helping others in our community.
